Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Who we are

London Wellness Club operates thelondonwellnessclub.com — a private members tennis and wellness club in London, UK. Contact: thelondonwellnessclub@gmail.com

What data we collect

On membership application we collect: first and last name, phone number, and Instagram handle. We do not collect payment data, sensitive data, or data from anyone under 18.

How we use it

Solely to review and respond to your membership application. Never used for marketing, advertising, or unrelated purposes.

Legal basis

Legitimate interest (evaluating applications) and implied consent at point of submission.

Who sees it

Only the London Wellness Club team directly involved in membership. Never sold or shared with third parties.

Retention

  • Unsuccessful applications: deleted within 30 days.
  • Active members: held for duration of membership, deleted within 60 days of cancellation.

Your rights (UK GDPR)

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Email thelondonwellnessclub@gmail.com — we respond within 30 days. You may also contact the ICO.

Cookies

No tracking cookies, analytics, or third-party scripts are used on this website.

Updates

We may update this policy. The date above reflects the latest version.
